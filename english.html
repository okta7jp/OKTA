"Pinch Points" of Living in Japan

Do you have these stresses? Tap to see details.

1. Water & Plumbing / 厨卫水电
  • "I don't understand the 'Oidaki' (reheating) function, so I drain and refill every day."不懂浴缸加热（追い焚き）功能，只能每天换水。
  • "I don't know how to clean the bidet nozzle. I'm worried it's dirty where I can't see."不知道智能马桶盖喷嘴怎么清理，很担心卫生。
  • "The kitchen sink is lower than in my home country. My back hurts every day."厨房水槽太矮，每天洗碗洗得腰酸背痛。
  • "I never know if it's okay to use soap in the water flowing over the toilet tank."总是在犹豫，能不能在马桶水箱上面的洗手池用肥皂。
  • "Smell from the washing machine drain? I don't know which parts to remove to clean."洗衣机排水口有异味，但不知道该拆哪儿清洗。
  • "sticky oil on the exhaust fan. I can't read the labels at the drugstore for cleaners."抽油烟机全是油污，在药妆店看不懂日文买哪种洗剂。
  • "Pink mold grows quickly in the bathroom floor grout."浴室地板缝隙里很快就长出了粉红色的霉菌。
  • "The hair catcher in the bathroom drain is too complicated to clean."浴室排水口的头发收集器结构太复杂，清理很头疼。
2. Electrical & Appliances / 电器设备
  • "The small green light on the wall switch? I keep pressing it to turn it off completely."不明白墙上开关那个绿色小灯，总想把它彻底关掉。
  • "Where do I connect the green earth/ground wire? Japanese outlets only have two prongs."插座只有两个孔，电器上多出来的绿色地线不知道接哪儿。
  • "The AC 'clean filter' light is on, but I'm afraid of breaking the cover."空调亮起清理滤网灯，但不敢拆外壳怕弄坏。
  • "Fast Japanese on the intercom? I pretend not to be home."门铃响了日语说太快听不懂，干脆假装不在家。
  • "Secretly turning off the '24h ventilation' to save electricity? (Don't do it!)"担心电费总是偷偷关掉浴室的24小时换气。
  • "Tripped breaker? Panic with too many switches in the distribution board."跳闸后打开配电箱，开关太多完全不知道推哪一个。
  • "Pulling the ceiling light string leaves a dim bulb on. How to turn it off?"拉电灯绳最后会亮起小夜灯，搞不懂怎么彻底关掉。
  • "I use the 'fish grill' under the stove only for storage space for frying pans."不懂燃气灶下面的烤鱼架怎么用，结果成了平底锅收纳位。
3. Fixtures & Interior / 门窗内装
  • "Rented apartment wall? I just lean my posters on the floor to avoid thumbtacks."不知道租房墙上能不能钉图钉，海报只能靠在地上。
  • "I shove cardboard boxes into the 'Oshiire' instead of futons."不懂壁橱（押し入れ）该怎么用，里面塞满了纸箱。
  • "Forcing the 'Fusuma' doors open because they don't slide smoothly."推拉门变得很难拉，不知道怎么修只能用蛮力。
  • "Bugs getting in? I didn't know the screen door has a correct opening side."不知道纱窗有固定开关方向，总是留缝进虫子。
  • "Deep indentations in the soft cushion floor from heavy furniture."地板是软塑胶材质，放了重家具后留下了很深的凹痕。
  • "I constantly kick my slippers with the room door (Inward or outward swing?)."不习惯房间门朝内开还是朝外开，经常踢飞拖鞋。
  • "Nitori curtains don't fit because there are too many types of hooks."宜得利买的窗帘挂钩种类太多，怎么也挂不好。
  • "Accidentally touching the sand-textured walls and sand falls off."不小心碰到和室沙土墙壁会掉沙子，清理起来很麻烦。
4. Exterior & Others / 室外其他
  • "Plastic garbage? How thoroughly do I need to wash it before throwing away?"垃圾分类太繁琐，不知道塑料盒要洗到什么程度。
  • "Bicycle valve is different from my country. My own air pump won't work."自行车气门芯形状不同（英式），自己的打气筒用不了。
  • "Too many flyers in the mailbox. Can I put a 'No Flyers' sticker?"信箱塞满广告，重要信件被埋，不知能不能贴拒绝贴纸。
  • "Afraid of Japanese bugs or snakes while pulling weeds in the garden."想拔院子里的杂草，但怕里面藏着毒虫或蛇不敢碰。
  • "Delivery locker PIN? I just request redelivery because it's too confusing."不太懂快递柜密码锁运作方式，只能麻烦快递员重送。
  • "Balcony drain is connected to neighbor. Nervous about my cleaning."阳台排水沟和隔壁连着，生怕邻居觉得打扫不干净。
  • "Earthquake prep? I don't know the correct way to install tension rods."想为了防震把家具固定一下，但不知道防震杆正确法。
  • "Frozen pipes in winter? I don't know what preventative measures to take."新闻说水管会冻裂，但完全不知道该怎么预防。
